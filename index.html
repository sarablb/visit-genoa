Visit Genoa

Discover culture and the sea in this fascinating city on the Ligurian Riviera.

Top three activities to do at Genoa

Visit the Aquarium of Genoa:
One of the largest in Europe, featuring dolphins, sharks, and hundreds of marine species in spectacular settings.

Get lost in the historic alleys:
Wander through the “caruggi,” the narrow medieval streets filled with artisan shops, cafés, and hidden gems.

Enjoy the view from Castelletto Belvedere:
Take in breathtaking panoramic views of the harbor and the city’s rooftops, especially at sunset.
